About InAmigos Foundation

Creating positive social impact through compassion, innovation, and community participation.

Who We Are

Founded on 23 September 2020, InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization committed to creating meaningful social change across India.

Our work focuses on education, healthcare, women empowerment, environmental conservation, animal welfare, youth development, and community service. Through dedicated volunteers and meaningful partnerships, we strive to build a more inclusive and sustainable society.

We believe that every act of kindness has the power to transform lives. By working together, we aim to create opportunities, spread awareness, and inspire positive action in communities.
